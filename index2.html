STIR FRY CHICKENS WITH MASH POTATO

Ingredients

  • 3/4 chicken
  • 2 kg potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon butter(you can add to your liking)
  • 1 bunch green onion
  • 2 red onions
  • 3 large carrots
  • 1 bunch of coriander(dhania in swahili)
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper
  • I bunch of pumpkin leaves
  • Dark soy sauce

Cooking Instructions

  1. Peel the potatoes whole.Wash them in running water.Put them in a pot,add water half the pot and salt. Cook on medium heat till tender enough to mash easily.
  2. Use a separate skillet, heat oil on medium heat.
    Put in your chicken cut into medium sizes.You can marinate the chicken with black pepper, soy sauce and garlic if you want it spicy.I wanted to go all natural on mine so i just added salt to the chicken. This enables it to take in the spices well as it cooks.
  3. Cover the chicken and let it cook for a while approximately 15 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, chop your onions, cut your carrots into juliennes and roughly chop your coriander.
  5. Pour the onions into the chicken,stir in and let it cook until translucent.
    Add the julienned carrots and stir occasionally till slightly cooked.Add in your coriander as garnish. Let it cook a bit before turning off the heat.
  6. For the mashed potatoes, add in the butter
  7. Serve your meal.Enjoy!
